    PRIVACY STATEMENT BEYOND MEASURE

    Beyond Measure, registered in the trade register of the Netherlands under number 78192552, is a sole proprietorship. Beyond Measure develops, produces and publishes software applications.
    With this Privacy Statement, we aim to provide clarity on how we deal with personal data.

    OUR PRIVACY POLICY

    We handle personal data with care and diligence. Personal data will be processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (AVG) and the Dutch General Data Protection Implementation Act (UAVG).

    WHOM DO WE PROCESS PERSONAL DATA FROM?

    Beyond Measure processes personal data from:
    - individuals who contact us via the get a quote form.
    In the above case, we are always the data controller.

    WHAT PERSONAL DATA ARE PROCESSED AND FOR WHAT PURPOSE?

    Beyond Measure processes the personal data of visitors from this website:
    - For contacting us for a possible collaboration
    For this purpose, we process:
    - personal data provided in the contact form, such as name, e-mail address, company name and position in the company
    Beyond Measure processes personal data of contact persons of suppliers and (potential) clients:
    - to maintain contact in relation to our services and business operations - to comply with any laws and regulations
    For this purpose, we process:
    - general contact information, such as name, email address, company name and position in the company.

    WHAT BASIS DO WE HAVE FOR PROCESSING PERSONAL DATA?

    We only process personal data that is necessary on the basis of the following bases from Article 6 of the AVG.
    i. Consent of data subject ii. Execution of the contract
    iii. Fulfillment of legal obligation
    iv. Justified interest

    HOW LONG WILL PERSONAL DATA BE KEPT?

    We do not keep personal data longer than necessary for the purpose of processing.

    WHAT RIGHTS DO YOU HAVE?

    When we process personal data about you, you have a number of rights as a data subject.

    1. Inspection: You have the right to access your personal data and can generally access the personal data we process about you within one month of your request to us.
    2. Correction: You can ask us to correct inaccuracies in your personal data.
    3. Deletion: You may ask us to delete personal data under certain circumstances. In this case, we will take reasonable steps to notify other processors who process personal data on our behalf that you have requested the deletion of links to and copies of your personal data.
    4. Objection to processing: If you object to certain processing of your personal data, for example emails containing offers.
    5. Restriction of processing: You may request us to restrict the processing of personal data under certain circumstances, for example if the accuracy of personal data is disputed by you.
    6. Transferability: You can ask us to obtain from us the personal data you have provided to us and which we store automatically, in a structured, common and machine-readable form, for example, in order to subsequently send it to a third party. This is called 'data portability'.
    7. Withdrawal of consent: Where you have given consent to use your personal data, you can withdraw your consent, which means that we will no longer process the personal data to which the consent applied.
    8. Making a complaint: You may lodge a complaint about the processing of your personal data by us with the Personal Data Authority.
    Exceptions may apply when exercising these rights, which means that you may not be able to exercise certain rights in all circumstances; for example, we may not always delete your data if we are required by law to retain it.

    HOW CAN YOU EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS? QUESTION OR COMPLAINT?

    To exercise your privacy rights, send us a letter or email with your request and a copy of your passport or ID card. Please make your BSN and passport photo illegible. We will respond within four weeks of receiving your letter.
    Also, if you have any questions or complaints about the way we handle your personal data, please contact:
    Beyond Measure
    Attn: Privacy Officer
